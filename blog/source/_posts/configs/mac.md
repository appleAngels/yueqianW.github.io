### 1、重装 Command Line Tools

> sudo rm -rf /Library/Developer/CommandLineTools

> xcode-select --install

`注意：xcode-select –install 安装失败,因为当前无法从软件更新服务器获得，不能安装该软件`

> https://developer.apple.com/download/more/
